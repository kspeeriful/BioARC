About

Why BioARC?

Biorepositories preserve time‑stamped, georeferenced records of biodiversity—the substrate for understanding emergence and spread of zoonoses. BioARC coordinates specimen‑based surveillance and bridges museums, public health, and communities to enable earlier detection and better response.

Mission

Strengthen adaptive, resilient capacity to detect and mitigate wildlife‑borne pathogens through standardized sampling, biobank networks, and open data.

Approach

Transdisciplinary teams integrate field sampling, specimen curation, molecular screening, ecological modeling, and risk communication.

Values

Equity, reciprocity, open science, and long‑term stewardship of specimens and data.
