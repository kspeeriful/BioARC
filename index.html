Project Description

BioARC (Biorepositories for Adaptive Resilience to Climate) is a multinational, transdisciplinary initiative focused on building early-warning systems for zoonotic diseases across the Americas. We are a network of biologists, virologists, social scientists, public health experts, epidemiologists, data scientists, museum scientists, and stakeholders. Our goal is to co-create infrastructure and best practices for sample collection, analysis, and sharing to improve pandemic preparedness powered by natural history collections and biorepositories.
