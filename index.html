Project Description

BioARC (Biorepositories for Adaptive Resilience to Climate) is a multinational, transdisciplinary initiative focused on building early-warning systems for zoonotic diseases across the Americas. Our goal is to co-create infrastructure and best practices for sample collection, analysis, and sharing to improve pandemic preparedness.
